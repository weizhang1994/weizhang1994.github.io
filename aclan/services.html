Frequently Asked Questions

How safe are your pesticide applications?
Pesticide use is pest specific. When using any pesticide the label is the law. These products are approved for use as per label instructions by Health Canada Pest Management Regulatory Agency. Product strength and application rates vary depending on the target pests. When applying a pesticide you can be sure, through proper application and following the label instructions we will keep your family and pets safe from harm. For some services we offer a toxic chemical free solution to pest control.
How can we be sure the guaranteed service will work?

We are proud to offer guaranteed pest control services. Pest problems elimination usually depend on a couple factors:

1. The size of the population contributes to the length of time needed to eliminite every last one. Key strategies includes keeping pests from food sources and harborage.

2. Our company has developed proprietary methods from years of experience in the industry. We have the skills and knowledge to offer a guaranteed service agreement.

3. Reintroduction of pests such as bed bugs and cockroaches can mean an ongoing issue. We provide strategies to prevent the reintroduction into your home just like our service technicians need to do to prevent accidentially taking home any unwanted guests.

Why should I hire a pest control company?
You could buy pesticides and attempt to fix your pest problem, or hire a professional and have them use pesticides they are familiar with instead. At Aclan Pest Control we know the products available in Canada and use them everyday. We also have the latest tools and equipment to apply them properly. Tools such as a B&G sprayers, Actisol machines, Exacticide dusters, infrared cameras and inspection probes make a huge difference in delivering results. The most important factor that makes a difference between doing it yourself and hiring a pest control company is familiarity. We see these pests almost everyday and typically when someone does something everyday over years they tend to get pretty good at it. we often see someone trying to fix a bed bug or cockroach problem themselves and actually making the situation more difficult to deal with. Most pesticides in Canada act as repellents and can cause pesticide resistant species to escape into walls and under floorboards. This means it may take longer to get rid of all of them.
How long after a treatment does it take to get results?
Results will vary according to the type of pest, treatment methods and products used. For example, immediate results can be seen when a wasps nest is removed. However, when using baits for ants, rodents, and cockroaches the process takes longer since the pests have to ingest the product for it to be effective. Your inspector and technician will outline what your expectations for your particular problem should be.
Why do bed bugs and other biting insects take more than one treatment to completely eliminiate the population?
Bed bugs, fleas and ticks are mobile pests and can be carried easily between locations or within a structure. Identifying the source of the infestation and taking the proper steps to ensure a re-infestation does not occur can take multiple service visits.
What about the smell?
There are several pesticides to choose from for individual pest problems and some smell less than others. Some pesticides barely smell at all. Microencapsulated formulas are very low odor where as emulsifided concentrates will usually smell like oil based paints. At Aclan Pest Control we don't use potentially deadly fumigants. Talk to your pest technician about making the right and informed decision. We know structural pesticides well.
