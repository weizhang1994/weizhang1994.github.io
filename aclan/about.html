BED BUGS

Bed bugs have become a common problem in Saskatchewan. We offer a 100% GUARANTEED SERVICE* to eradicate them and keep you bite free from day one.

*Some Conditions Apply

What You Can Do

With traditional methods like chemical treatments and heat treatments bed bugs can be very difficult to eradicate. We use a combination of methods that have proven results and will get you bite free overnight plus make sure to kill every last one. We offer preperation services and pesticide free services for bed bugs. Speak with our team and use the following list for helpful things you can do right away. Please note; these may or may not not be necessary:

  • 1. Read The Bed Bug Preparation Sheet and start preparing if necessary.
  • 2. You can launder your materials tht may be suspect of harboring bed bugs including; bedding and linens, clothing, curtains, backpacks, stuffed toys, etc. (heat from a conventional dryer will kill all life stages of bedbugs)
  • 3. Go through your personal belongings that may be suspect of harboring bed bugs; anything that can't be laundered can be thoroughly inspected for signs of bedbugs. Speak with our team for more information.
  • 4. You can purchase bed bug mattress encasements for both mattresses and box springs through Aclan Pest Control.

All professional products are applied by adhering to the product label instructions for specific pests and pest management.

We believe in providing a completely transparent approach to delivering services to our customers. If pesticides are used we can provide the product names for a particular service. That way we can ensure you know exactly what your buying.
